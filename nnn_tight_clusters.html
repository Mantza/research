NNN Tight Coordination Clusters

Full-database Jaccard similarity analysis across 2.44M Community Notes — 365K tweets with NNN votes

Method: Unlike the previous analysis (which only looked at "convoy tweets" with 5+ NNN authors), this scan computed pairwise Jaccard similarity for all 27,832 active NNN authors across every tweet in the database. Clusters are groups of 3+ authors where each pair shares ≥3 tweets and Jaccard similarity ≥0.05. This catches tight coordination on low-profile tweets that the convoy approach missed entirely.

Result: 8 clusters found. Most operate on tweets outside the top-1000 most-NNN'd tweets — they're working under the radar.
